About the Dashboard
This dashboard was compiled by UNOCHA-EA to visualize the humanitarian scenario of the Eastern Africa region. For comment/feedback please contact okombo@un.org While we try to keep the data as accurate and up to date as possible, some of the information may not be so much up to date.
